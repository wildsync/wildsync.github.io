The Problem

HubSpot's native sync wasn't built for real teams

If you've hit these walls, you're not alone. These are the top complaints from revenue teams using HubSpot + Google Calendar.

⚠

One calendar per user

HubSpot only syncs a single Google Calendar. If your team uses separate calendars for work, personal, or projects, availability is wrong and double-bookings happen.

⚠

Sync delays cause chaos

Changes in Google Calendar can take minutes to reflect in HubSpot. In that window, prospects book on top of existing meetings, creating conflicts your reps have to untangle.

⚠

No smart routing

Round-robin is all you get. No territory-based routing, no weighted distribution, no skills-based matching. Complex sales teams need more than a coin flip.

⚠

Zero meeting analytics

How many meetings actually led to revenue? HubSpot logs meetings but doesn't connect the dots between calendar activity and pipeline outcomes.

⚠

No buffer or daily caps

Reps get stacked back-to-back with no breathing room. No minimum notice, no cooldowns, no maximum meetings per day. Burnout by design.

⚠

Painful rescheduling

Prospects can't easily reschedule from confirmation emails. They email your rep, who manually juggles calendars. It's 2026 — this should be one click.
